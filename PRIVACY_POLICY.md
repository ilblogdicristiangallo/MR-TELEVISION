# Privacy Policy for MR-TELEVISION

**Last Updated:** February 24, 2026

MR-TELEVISION ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile and TV application.

### 1. No Content Provider Disclaimer
MR-TELEVISION is a pure media player application. We DO NOT provide, host, or sell any TV channels, playlists, VOD content, or subscriptions. Users must provide their own content (M3U playlists or Xtream Codes) from legal providers.

### 2. Information Collection and Use
We do not collect, store, or share any personal identification information. 
- **Playlists:** The IPTV URLs, usernames, and passwords you enter are stored locally on your device to enable the application to function. They are never sent to our servers.
- **VPN Profiles:** In the PRO version, VPN configuration files (.conf) are stored locally on your device to establish a secure connection via the WireGuard protocol.

### 3. Device Permissions
To provide its features, the app requires the following permissions:
- **Internet Access:** Required to stream video content from your provided links.
- **Storage/Media Access:** Required to load local .conf files for the VPN manager or local M3U files.
- **Network State:** Required to check if the device is connected to the internet.
- **VPN Service:** Required for the integrated VPN manager (PRO version only) to encrypt your traffic.

### 4. Third-Party Services
Our app does not use third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. However, when you stream content, your IPTV provider may see your IP address, just as they would in any other player. We recommend using a VPN to protect your privacy.

### 5. Security
We value your trust in providing us your configuration data, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. But remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

### 6. Children’s Privacy
Our application is rated for everyone. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children.

### 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
